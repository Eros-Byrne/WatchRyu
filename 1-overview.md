# Overview

Student ID: `c24056128`

**WatchRyu** is a high-performance Android application designed for anime enthusiasts to manage their library with professional-grade tools. Developed for the Pixel 3a (Android 15), the app provides a comprehensive tracking suite across seven distinct categories, including "Currently Watching" and "On-Hold." 

The motivation for WatchRyu was to bridge the gap between simple lists and data-heavy platforms like MyAnimeList by offering a minimalist, local-first experience. Users can discover shows via the Jikan API (Top, Seasonal, and Upcoming), track granular episode progress, and save personal reviews directly to a local SQLite database.

Technically, WatchRyu is a masterclass in Modern Android Development (MAD). It utilizes a multi-layered MVVM architecture to ensure total state preservation during configuration changes. By integrating Retrofit for networking, Room for persistence, and DataStore for both accessibility settings and cache-aware timestamps, the app eliminates all deprecated practices. The inclusion of dynamic Pie Chart analytics and MAL-compatible XML portability demonstrates a sophisticated understanding of data management and user experience. WatchRyu isn't just a list; it’s a robust, branded product that offers users complete control over their viewing history with a focus on privacy, speed, and accessibility. (203 words)
